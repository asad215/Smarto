Privacy Policy

At Smarto.Space, we are committed to protecting your privacy and personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in relation to our Android application that provides AI-powered WhatsApp customer service solutions.

Information We Collect

Our app does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users. It only processes incoming WhatsApp messages locally and responds based on pre-configured logic or AI-powered assistance.

Use of Information

The application uses temporary session memory to personalize responses and deliver WhatsApp customer service automation. No data is stored or transmitted to external servers without your consent.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect any data processed by our app. Your device data remains private and secure. We do not share it with third-party services.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Any major changes will be communicated via our official website.
